The era of experimental AI agents is officially evolving into a reality of robust, production-grade systems. Microsoft has announced that the **Agent Framework is now version 1.0** for both .NET and Python, providing developers with the stable APIs and **Long-Term Support (LTS)** commitment required to build enterprise-scale solutions with complete confidence.

This milestone release is a game-changer for those tackling complex automation. Version 1.0 introduces powerful capabilities for **multi-agent orchestration**, enabling diverse AI entities to collaborate seamlessly on intricate tasks. With *multi-provider model support* and the groundbreaking *A2A (Agent-to-Agent)* protocol for cross-runtime interoperability, your agents can now communicate across different environments, ensuring your AI ecosystem remains flexible, scalable, and future-proof.
