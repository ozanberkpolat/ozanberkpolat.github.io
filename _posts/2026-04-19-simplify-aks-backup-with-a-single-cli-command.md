Azure Backup just got a whole lot easier for Kubernetes users. Microsoft has introduced a streamlined experience to configure backup for Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS) clusters using just one Azure CLI command—eliminating the need for multiple manual steps and complex installations.

This update significantly reduces the overhead of enabling AKS backup, making it faster and more accessible for developers and administrators. With this enhancement, you can now protect your Kubernetes workloads more efficiently without diving into intricate configurations or third-party tools.
