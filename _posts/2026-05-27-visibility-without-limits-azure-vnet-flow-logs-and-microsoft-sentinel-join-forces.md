Is your network traffic a blind spot in your security strategy? In modern cloud environments, visibility is the difference between a minor incident and a total breach. Identifying lateral movement and unauthorized exfiltration requires deep, real-time insights into every packet flow within your infrastructure.

With the **Azure Virtual Network (VNet) flow logs connector for Microsoft Sentinel** now Generally Available, the wait for seamless integration is over. This update empowers security operations (SecOps) teams to export and analyze traffic data directly within their existing workflows. By leveraging these rich telemetry streams, you can now correlate network activity with other security signals to build a **unified defense posture** that responds to threats in real-time.
