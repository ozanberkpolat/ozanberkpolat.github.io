Tired of choosing between powerful analytics and network security? We've got great news. Azure Cosmos DB Mirroring in Microsoft Fabric now supports private endpoints, giving you the best of both worlds.

This GA release lets you unlock real-time operational insights while maintaining your enhanced security posture. No more exposing sensitive data to the public internet—mirror your Cosmos DB data directly into Fabric's analytics engine through private network connections. Your security team will love it, and your data scientists will too.

Ready to secure your analytics pipeline? [Dive into the full technical breakdown here](https://azure.microsoft.com/updates?id=558836)
