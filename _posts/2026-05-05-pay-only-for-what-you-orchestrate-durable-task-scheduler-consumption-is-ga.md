Why pay for idle infrastructure when your logic isn't running? The **Durable Task Scheduler Consumption SKU** has officially reached General Availability, offering a paradigm shift for developers building resilient, event-driven systems. By stripping away the need for storage management and capacity planning, Azure is making it easier than ever to deploy complex, long-running workflows without the traditional operational overhead.

The core value is simple: **Zero idle costs.** This update allows you to run durable workflows and AI agent orchestrations on a purely pay-per-use basis. You no longer have to worry about managing backend storage or over-provisioning for peak loads. It is a leaner, faster, and more cost-effective way to handle orchestration at scale, letting you focus on the logic that drives your business.
