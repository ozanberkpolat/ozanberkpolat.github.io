Tired of complex data pipelines and redundant data copies? Big news for everyone leveraging Azure's powerful data ecosystem! We're thrilled to announce a significant update that simplifies your data architecture and accelerates insights.

*Azure Databricks* now officially supports **General Availability** for native read access to data stored in *Microsoft OneLake* via *Unity Catalog*. This groundbreaking integration means you can seamlessly query and analyze your OneLake data directly, **without any data movement or duplication**. Imagine the time saved, the costs cut, and the dramatic boost in data agility! This is a monumental step towards truly unified and efficient data operations.
