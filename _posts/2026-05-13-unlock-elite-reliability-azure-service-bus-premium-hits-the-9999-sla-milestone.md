Is your messaging infrastructure resilient enough to handle the unexpected? In today’s enterprise landscape, even a few minutes of downtime can result in significant operational friction. Azure is stepping up to the plate by significantly raising the bar for its **Service Bus Premium** tier, ensuring your mission-critical data flows without interruption.

Starting **May 1, 2026**, all Azure Service Bus Premium namespaces deployed in regions with *Availability Zone* support now qualify for a **99.99% uptime SLA**. This isn't just a minor update; it's a major commitment to reliability. By leveraging physically separate locations within an Azure region, this tier now provides the robust fault tolerance required for the world's most demanding workloads, offering architects greater peace of mind and more resilient messaging backbones.
