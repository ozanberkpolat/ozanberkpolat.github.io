In the world of cloud-scale messaging, the line between accessibility and security is often thin. Azure Event Hubs serves as a cornerstone for modern data streams, but managing public access risks has historically required complex workarounds. That ends today.

The official support for **Network Security Perimeter (NSP)** marks a significant evolution in how we protect cloud-native resources. Organizations can now define **logical network isolation boundaries** specifically for their PaaS assets. By implementing these perimeter-based access rules, you gain granular control over inbound traffic, effectively shielding your event streams from the public internet while maintaining seamless connectivity within your trusted architecture. This update is essential for enterprises requiring strict compliance and hardened network postures for their event-driven pipelines.
