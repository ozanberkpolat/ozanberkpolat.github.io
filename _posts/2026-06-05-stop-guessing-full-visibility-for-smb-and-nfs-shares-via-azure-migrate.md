**Migration complexity** just met its match. When moving legacy workloads to the cloud, one of the biggest hurdles is the opaque nature of on-premises storage. For far too long, administrators had to manually inventory file shares across disparate Windows and Linux servers, often leading to sizing errors and missed dependencies.

That era is officially over. **Azure Migrate** has expanded its capabilities to generally support the **discovery and assessment of SMB and NFS file shares**. This update provides a unified, data-driven dashboard that illuminates your entire file share environment at scale. By surfacing granular insights into usage and configuration, you can now build a precise, risk-free path to Azure storage solutions without the manual overhead.

Take the guesswork out of your infrastructure modernization and gain the clarity your migration strategy deserves.
