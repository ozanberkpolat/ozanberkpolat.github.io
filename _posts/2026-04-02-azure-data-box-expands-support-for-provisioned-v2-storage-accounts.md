date: 2026-04-02

Azure Data Box just got a major upgrade! Microsoft has expanded its capabilities to support ingestion of data directly into Azure Files Provisioned v2 storage accounts. This new feature empowers customers with greater flexibility by allowing them to provision and pay for specific capacity, IOPS, and throughput values—tailoring storage performance to their exact needs.

With this enhancement, Azure Data Box becomes an even more powerful tool for large-scale data migration and backup scenarios. Whether you're moving massive datasets to the cloud or optimizing your storage costs, this update ensures you can now leverage the advanced billing model of Provisioned v2 accounts seamlessly. It’s a game-changer for businesses looking to balance performance and cost efficiency.
