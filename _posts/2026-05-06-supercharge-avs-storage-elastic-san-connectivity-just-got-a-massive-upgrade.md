Managing storage in the cloud shouldn't feel like a complex networking puzzle. For those running **Azure VMware Solution (AVS) Gen2 Private Clouds**, the barrier to high-performance storage just vanished. The integration of **Elastic SAN datastores** now offers a streamlined path to scaling your environment without the legacy baggage of complicated configurations.

The core value of this update lies in its radical simplicity: you no longer need an **ExpressRoute gateway** to bridge your storage. By utilizing a **single Private Endpoint**, administrators can achieve *improved performance* and *simpler connectivity* in minutes. This shift not only reduces infrastructure overhead but also minimizes the technical friction typically associated with large-scale datastore deployments. It is a definitive win for teams looking to optimize their Gen2 environments with less complexity and more throughput.
