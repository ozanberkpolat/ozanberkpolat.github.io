The serverless landscape just received a massive performance injection. **Azure Functions support for Node.js 24 is now Generally Available**, bringing the latest runtime capabilities to your event-driven architectures. In an era where every millisecond of execution time matters, staying at the forefront of the JavaScript ecosystem isn't just an advantage—it’s a necessity for building high-performance, modern applications.

With this update, you can seamlessly develop locally and deploy to any supported Azure Functions plan on both **Linux and Windows**. Most notably, this includes full support for the **Flex Consumption plan**, allowing you to pair the latest Node.js optimizations with Azure's most flexible and rapid scaling logic. Whether you are leveraging new V8 engine features or simply looking for improved runtime stability, this release ensures your cloud-native toolbelt remains cutting-edge.
