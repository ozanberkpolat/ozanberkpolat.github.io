Does your architecture diagram actually reflect your system's survival rate? Most engineering teams possess the necessary artifacts—data flows, sequence diagrams, and incident logs—but lack a consistent way to connect them into a **measurable resilience model**. The gap between "designing for reliability" and "actually operationalizing it" is often where critical outages occur.

The **Application Resilience Framework Tool** bridges this divide by transforming your existing artifacts—including Mermaid files and raw images—into a structured lifecycle of risk identification and health modeling. By automating **Failure Mode Analysis (FMA)** and prioritizing risks using **Risk Priority Values (RPV)**, this framework helps you move beyond static documentation. You can now generate prioritized mitigation plans, chaos test strategies, and real-time health signals that align directly with the **Azure Well-Architected Framework** (WAF).

Ready to turn your architecture context into a structured, measurable defense?
