Azure Data Box just got a major trust boost. With every completed order, you now receive an automatically generated, downloadable Secure Erasure Certificate—proof that your data has been wiped clean in strict compliance with NIST 800-88 Revision 2 standards. No more manual tracking or guesswork.

This enhancement streamlines compliance workflows and strengthens audit readiness, giving IT and security teams instant, verifiable assurance that sensitive data is irretrievably erased before device return. It's a small change with big implications for data governance and regulatory confidence.
