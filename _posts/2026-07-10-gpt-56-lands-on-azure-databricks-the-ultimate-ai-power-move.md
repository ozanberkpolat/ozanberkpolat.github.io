The next evolution of generative AI is officially here. **OpenAI GPT-5.6** has arrived on **Azure Databricks**, bridging the gap between massive-scale data engineering and frontier-model intelligence. For enterprises looking to move beyond simple pilots into production-grade AI, this integration represents a critical turning point in how large language models are deployed at scale.

By leveraging **Microsoft Foundry**, users can now access GPT-5.6 directly through **Model Serving Endpoints** within the Databricks ecosystem. This seamless integration allows your team to securely build, deploy, and manage advanced AI applications within a unified environment. Whether you are automating complex workflows or developing bespoke customer experiences, the combination of Foundry’s procurement flexibility and Databricks’ robust governance makes this the most secure way to harness the power of OpenAI’s latest iteration.
