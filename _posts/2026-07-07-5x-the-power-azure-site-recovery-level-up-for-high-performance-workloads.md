Is your disaster recovery strategy struggling to keep up with your most data-intensive applications? For organizations running high-transaction databases and heavy IOPS workloads, data churn has often been the bottleneck that limits protection options. **Azure Site Recovery just shattered those limits.**

Microsoft has announced a massive enhancement: Azure Site Recovery now supports up to **5x churn**, allowing for a staggering **500 MB/s per VM**. This update is a game-changer for enterprises that need to ensure robust business continuity for their most demanding environments. By significantly increasing throughput limits, you can now confidently replicate high-performance workloads without the risk of synchronization lag or data protection gaps.
