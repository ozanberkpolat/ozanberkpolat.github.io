Azure Service Bus is now fortified with Network Security Perimeter (NSP) in general availability—a game-changing security layer that creates a virtual fortress around your namespaces. By establishing a logical network boundary, NSP blocks all unauthorized public access, giving your data the protection it deserves in today's threat landscape.

This isn't just another checkbox feature—it's a strategic move to secure your cloud communications at the network level. NSP works seamlessly with your existing Azure PaaS resources, ensuring that only trusted entities can interact with your Service Bus. Whether you're handling sensitive transactions or mission-critical messaging, this update empowers you to take control of your security posture without sacrificing performance.
