The boundary between raw speed and high-level reasoning has just blurred. **Azure Databricks** now supports **Anthropic Claude Sonnet 5** through AI Model Serving, bringing one of the industry's most sophisticated "agentic" models directly into your data ecosystem. In a landscape where performance often comes at a premium, this update offers a rare combination of **near-Opus-level intelligence** and the cost-efficiency required for production-scale deployment.

Claude Sonnet 5 is specifically designed for speed and complex autonomy, making it an ideal candidate for building advanced AI agents that can reason through multi-step tasks within your existing Databricks environment. By integrating this model into AI Model Serving, Azure provides a seamless path to deploy state-of-the-art LLMs with enterprise-grade reliability and lightning-fast response times. It is the perfect balance for developers who refuse to choose between **intelligence** and **efficiency**.
