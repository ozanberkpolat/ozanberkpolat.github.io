The wait is over—Azure Premium SSD v2 is now live in South India, a region that doesn't support Availability Zones. This next-gen block storage option is built for high-performance workloads, delivering sub-millisecond latencies and a compelling price-to-performance ratio.

Whether you're running latency-sensitive databases or high-throughput applications, Premium SSD v2 offers the reliability and speed you need without breaking the bank. It's the perfect fit for general-purpose workloads looking to squeeze every ounce of performance from their VMs.
