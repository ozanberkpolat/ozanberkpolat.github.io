Are you tired of context-switching between diagramming tools, pricing calculators, and documentation? Designing Azure architectures often feels like a fragmented puzzle, but a new open-source project is changing the game. By leveraging the power of **Azure OpenAI**, architects can now transform plain-English prompts into fully realized infrastructure diagrams, complete with cost estimates and deployment-ready Bicep templates.

The **Azure Architecture Diagram Builder** unifies the entire lifecycle in one browser-based experience. Beyond mere visuals, it performs **Well-Architected Framework (WAF) validation**, enables side-by-side multi-model comparisons, and even features a photorealistic avatar presenter to narrate your architectural workflows. Whether you're sketching from scratch, importing existing ARM templates, or whiteboarding a new concept, this tool streamlines the path from initial thought to production.
