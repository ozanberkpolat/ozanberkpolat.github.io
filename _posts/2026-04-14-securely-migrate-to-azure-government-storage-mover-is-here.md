U.S. government agencies and partners, are you looking for a streamlined, secure way to move *massive* file datasets to the cloud? The wait is over! **Azure Storage Mover** is now available in Azure Government, offering a fully managed service designed to simplify and accelerate your data migration journey.

This means you can now leverage a robust, reliable solution to transfer large-scale file data without the complexities of manual processes or the security concerns of alternative methods. Storage Mover handles the heavy lifting, ensuring a smooth and compliant transition to Azure Government.
