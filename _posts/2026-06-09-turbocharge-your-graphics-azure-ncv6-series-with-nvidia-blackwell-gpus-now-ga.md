**The next generation of high-performance visual computing has arrived.** As organizations push the boundaries of real-time rendering, complex simulation, and AI-accelerated design, the demand for cutting-edge hardware has never been higher. Azure is answering the call by announcing the **General Availability** of the NCv6-series virtual machines in the Southeast Asia and West US 2 regions.

These VMs represent a significant leap in cloud-based graphical power. Driven by the **NVIDIA RTX PRO 6000 Blackwell Server Edition GPUs**, each instance boasts a massive **96 GB of dedicated memory**. This provides the necessary headroom for professional visualization, high-end rendering, and memory-intensive compute tasks that demand extreme throughput. If your workloads require the peak of NVIDIA's Blackwell architecture, the wait is officially over.
