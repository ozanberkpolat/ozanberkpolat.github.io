**European cloud expansion just reached a significant milestone for enterprise Kubernetes users.** For organizations operating within the DACH region, the wait for locally-hosted, enterprise-grade managed services is over. As digital sovereignty becomes a non-negotiable requirement, this regional launch provides the perfect intersection of managed agility and local control.

Azure Red Hat OpenShift (ARO) is now **generally available** in the **Austria East** region. This launch provides a robust foundation for mission-critical applications by incorporating **three availability zones**, ensuring your operations remain resilient and scalable even under pressure. Most importantly, this update addresses the critical requirement of **in-country data residency**. Organizations can now ensure that their data remains stored within Austrian borders, satisfying strict local regulatory standards while leveraging the full power of the Red Hat OpenShift ecosystem.
