The next generation of cloud performance has officially arrived. Microsoft Azure has announced the general availability of the **Dl/D/E v7 Virtual Machines**, powered by the latest **Intel® Xeon® 6 (Granite Rapids)** processors. For organizations looking to scale their infrastructure without compromising on speed or efficiency, this hardware refresh marks a significant milestone in the Azure ecosystem.

The technical advantage is clear: these new general-purpose and memory-optimized VMs deliver up to **20% better general compute performance**. Whether you are managing high-demand databases, enterprise-grade applications, or complex web services, the v7 series provides the architecture needed to maximize throughput. With Intel's newest silicon under the hood, these instances are designed to handle your most resource-intensive workloads with ease.
