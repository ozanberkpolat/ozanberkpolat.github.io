What happens when your global load balancer experiences an outage? Even Azure Front Door (AFD), a critical component for countless **mission-critical applications**, can face rare but impactful platform incidents. Don't let your services be caught off guard.

Following critical lessons from October 2025 incidents, Microsoft has significantly hardened the AFD platform. But *your* architecture is key to true resilience. This essential article outlines **proven architectural patterns**—from robust multi-CDN strategies to intelligent DNS failover with Application Gateway—designed to maintain business continuity and ensure **unshakeable availability** when global routing services are unavailable. Understand the trade-offs and select the optimal strategy for your high-stakes workloads.
