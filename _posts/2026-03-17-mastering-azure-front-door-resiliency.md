Azure Front Door (AFD) resiliency requires strategic design. Field lessons highlight *multi-region architectures* for disaster recovery, precise *health probe configurations*, and leveraging *origin groups* for seamless failover. Effective *caching* and understanding traffic routing priorities are crucial for ensuring high availability and minimal downtime across global deployments. Learn more: [Resiliency Patterns for Azure Front Door](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/azure-architecture-blog/resiliency-patterns-for-azure-front-door-field-lessons/ba-p/4501252)
