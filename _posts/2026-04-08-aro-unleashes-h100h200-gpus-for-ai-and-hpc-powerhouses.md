**Big news for AI and HPC workloads:** Azure Red Hat OpenShift (ARO) now supports NVIDIA H100 and H200 GPU-based Azure Virtual Machine SKUs. This means you can now run large-scale, GPU-accelerated AI, machine learning, and high-performance computing workloads on a fully managed OpenShift service—without the operational overhead.

With these cutting-edge GPUs, ARO delivers unmatched performance for training complex models, processing massive datasets, and executing compute-intensive simulations. Whether you're scaling AI pipelines or pushing the boundaries of HPC, this update gives you the power to innovate faster and more efficiently.
