Why settle for static charts when you can harness the full power of **Grafana’s** open visualization platform natively within the **Azure Portal**? The wait is over—Azure Monitor dashboards with Grafana are now **Generally Available**, marking a significant milestone for developers and operators who demand high-fidelity observability.

This deep integration allows you to build, customize, and share professional-grade dashboards without leaving your cloud environment. By leveraging Grafana’s *composable* architecture, you can now unify metrics across disparate sources into a single, cohesive pane of glass. Whether you are troubleshooting a production outage or tracking long-term performance, these dashboards provide the clarity and flexibility needed to make informed decisions faster.

Ready to transform your telemetry into actionable insights?
