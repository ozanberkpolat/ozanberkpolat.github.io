Protecting sensitive information isn't just a compliance requirement—it's a critical pillar of modern data integrity. With the latest update to **Azure AI Language**, managing Personally Identifiable Information (PII) within complex documents has become more intuitive than ever.

The new **Microsoft Foundry NextGen portal** now hosts a dedicated playground for document-based PII detection. This sandbox allows developers and security architects to upload sample documents and witness advanced redaction algorithms in action. By visualizing the *redacted output* in real-time, teams can validate detection accuracy and refine their data protection strategies without writing a single line of code. It is a powerful, low-friction environment designed to bridge the gap between **compliance policy** and **technical implementation**.
