The era of experimental AI agents is evolving into a reality of robust, enterprise-scale automation. Microsoft has officially announced **Agent Framework 1.0** for both .NET and Python, signaling a major leap forward for developers building autonomous systems. With this release, the framework moves beyond preview status, offering the stability and reliability required for mission-critical applications.

This milestone is packed with features designed for complex environments. Version 1.0 delivers **stable APIs** and a long-term support (LTS) commitment, ensuring your development efforts remain future-proof. Technical highlights include sophisticated **multi-agent orchestration**, support for diverse model providers, and seamless **cross-runtime interoperability (A2A)**. This means agents can now collaborate across different runtimes, breaking down silos and enabling truly flexible AI architectures.
