It doesn’t take long for AI agents to get out of hand. In the race to innovate, enterprises often find themselves buried under a **"sprawl" of autonomous systems**, leading to uncomfortable questions about ownership, data access, and runaway token budgets. When developers can deploy an agent in minutes, the challenge shifts from *building* to *governing*—shifting the focus from model quality to operational stability.

This new reference architecture solves the chaos by introducing a **multi-region AI agent landing zone** on Azure. The core principle is simple: **separate control from execution.** By layering the *Azure AI Gateway* for request-level limits, the *Foundry Control Plane* for behavioral evaluation, and *Microsoft Agent 365* for tenant-wide identity and compliance, IT teams can transform AI from a fragmented experiment into a secure, observable, and first-class workload.
