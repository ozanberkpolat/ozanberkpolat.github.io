Is your data residency strategy hitting a roadblock in Southeast Asia? The wait is officially over. Microsoft has just announced that **SQL Server on Azure Virtual Machines** is now generally available in both the **Malaysia West** and **Indonesia Central** regions.

This expansion is a game-changer for regional performance, allowing you to deploy high-availability database workloads significantly closer to your end-users. By reducing latency and ensuring compliance with local data residency requirements, your infrastructure can now scale with confidence in these emerging digital hubs. Whether you are migrating legacy systems or architecting new cloud-native solutions, this update provides the regional footprint you need to optimize your Azure footprint.
