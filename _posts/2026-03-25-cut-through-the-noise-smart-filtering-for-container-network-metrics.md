Struggling to make sense of overwhelming network metrics? When container environments generate massive volumes of observability data, finding what truly matters becomes a needle-in-a-haystack problem. That's why Azure Container Networking Services (ACNS) now offers container network metrics filtering—giving operators precise control over which metrics they track and analyze.

This powerful feature lets you focus on operationally relevant data by filtering out the noise, dramatically reducing the volume of metrics you need to process while ensuring you never miss critical signals. Whether you're monitoring microservices architectures or complex containerized applications, this update puts you back in control of your observability strategy.
