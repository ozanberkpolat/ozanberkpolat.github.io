Protecting your mission-critical data just became more efficient and granular. For organizations relying on high-performance storage, the ability to safeguard individual assets without the complexity of full-scale replication is a game-changer. Azure Elastic SAN is stepping up its game by introducing **single volume snapshots**, a long-awaited feature that brings surgical precision to your backup and recovery strategies.

This update introduces **incremental, point-in-time backups** that capture only the data changed since your last snapshot. By residing directly within the SAN, these snapshots enable lightning-fast deployment of new volumes—perfect for rapid recovery or spinning up dev/test environments. It is a powerful way to reduce storage overhead while significantly enhancing your data resiliency and reducing your Recovery Time Objective (RTO).
