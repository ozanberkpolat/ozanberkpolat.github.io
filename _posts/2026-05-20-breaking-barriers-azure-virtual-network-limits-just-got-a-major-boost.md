Is your network architecture feeling the pinch? Managing complex, large-scale cloud environments just got significantly easier. Microsoft has officially expanded the default platform limits for Azure Virtual Network, providing the much-needed headroom for organizations scaling their cloud infrastructure.

This update delivers a massive increase in capacity: you can now leverage up to **2,000 security rules per NSG**, **6,000 addresses or ports per rule**, and **1,000 routes per route table**. Whether you are managing sprawling enterprise subnets or intricate security policies, these elevated thresholds reduce the complexity of your deployment configurations and eliminate the need for cumbersome workarounds.
