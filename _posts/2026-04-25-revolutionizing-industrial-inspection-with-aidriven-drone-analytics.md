> AI‑driven inspection is reshaping safety‑critical workflows.

Manual framing of thousands of bolts across noisy factory floors has long forced engineers to stare at drone footage frame‑by‑frame, a slow, error‑prone process. The result? Missed anomalies, inconsistent measurements, and ramp‑up costs that scale poorly.

Enter a hybrid AI pipeline that marries deterministic computer‑vision models—delivering repeatable bolt detection, alignment metrics, and defect scoring—with Generative AI that cross‑references frames for context‑aware validation. The architecture runs on Azure, from Blob‑triggered Functions through Azure AI Vision, Azure ML, and Azure OpenAI, feeding structured results into Cosmos DB and Power BI dashboards. The net effect: higher accuracy, fewer false positives, and an auditable, scalable inspection loop.
